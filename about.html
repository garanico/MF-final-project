Hi, I’m Rachel Aranico! I’m a people loving photographer, based in beautiful San Diego, CA.
My philosphy is that there’s no pressure for that perfect photo. I’m all about documenting the big moments, the small ones in-between, and the ones that were total accidents. That's where the magic happens!
